WEN Abundance

WEN Abundance was set up in 2007 and is part West Ealing Neighbours, a local residents group.   We have around 40 volunteers and pick from private back gardens as well as green open spaces in the West Ealing/Ealing and Hanwell area. The fruit is put to good use by: Giving away to the local soup kitchen Free fruit box distribution scheme Making jams, chutneys, juice and cordials to sell at local fairs - all monies are ploughed back into the project to buy equipment Work with local social enterprises/housing associations on related projects We also have a small Orchard in W13 that we are currently renovating with help from The London Orchard Project WEN Abundance works on a purely voluntary basis and we always welcome volunteers, even if they can only help occasionally.
