Orchard Cardiff

Orchard Cardiff aims to bring together people interested in all abundance activities across Cardiff.As well as promoting a fruit map, arranging harvesting activities in the autumn and our own Apple Day in Clare Gardens, Orchard Cardiff are also really keen to support any other community groups across the city to do the same, developing harvesting activities on an increasingly local level. To find out more, visit our Facebook page (Orchard Cardiff) or contact jenny@orchardcardiff.co.uk to be put on our e-mailing list. We have a website that currently works as a portal to our map and Facebook page. If you fancy helping to improve our website, please get in touch.
