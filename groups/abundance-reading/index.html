Abundance Reading

Based in Reading, we've been running since around 2010. We harvest apples and pears from local trees (either on public land, or in gardens by request). And provide apples for events like the Reading Town Meal. Surplus fruit (and any vegetables) are donated to a local drop-in centre for the needy and community caf√©s. We're a project of Transition Town Reading, and have a community apple crusher and press which are available for community groups and events. Transition Town Reading are involved in planting orchards on public land around Reading, and we help to tend those orchards.
