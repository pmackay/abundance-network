Commonwealth Orchard / Scottish Orchards

Scottish Orchards is a network of orchard enthusiasts, schools, community projects and growers across Scotland. We can deliver training, skills and tool sharing, and help to create the conditions for an Abundant Scotland. www.scottishorchards,.com John Hancox, Chair of Scottish Orchards also is Director of the Commonwealth Orchard which has planted many community orchards across Scotland, delivers training and supplies fruit trees to schools and community groups. He pioneered an Abundance type project in Glasgow starting in 2008 and has helped establish the Abundance Glasgow South Side project with Locavore. www.commonwealthorchard.com John also can supply specialist fruit trees via www.scottishfruittrees.com (may also suit North England and other locations where hardy trees are needed!)
