Abundance East Herefordshire

With so many families struggling to cope with rising food prices, leaving fruit to rot has surely got to be a crime! Help your local community get in touch with nature, whilst at the same time providing them with fresh, wholesome produce. We would love to build a local, thriving Abundance group in East Herefordshire, strengthening community and helping each other. If you would like to spend time helping people gather gluts of fruit from their gardens, please get in touch. Or if indeed you have an unmanageable fruit supply, or just a little going spare, please do contact us!
