Abundance Glasgow

The group made up of volunteers from various different areas in the city is in its infancy. We are discovering the joys of urban fruit harvesting. Mainly having a harvest and a workshop each weekend. Looking to get more and more fruit so we can share it around. Based at Locavore (the local food hub in the Southside of Glasgow www.glasgowlocavore.org). We also have some new toys to help us along the way: a press+crusher, fruit pickers, dehydrator (for some tasty apple crisps) and more.

Website: http://www.glasgowlocavore.org
